Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before the rental start date. If you cancel within 24 hours of the rental start date, there will be a cancellation fee.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for an additional fee. You can select this option during the booking process.
